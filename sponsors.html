Acknowledgement

We'd like to thank the following organizations for their kind support and various contributions, without which our projects would not have been possible.

Exclusive Diamond Sponsor

The Daniel Family Foundation

The Daniel Family Foundation is a philanthropic foundation that funds leading edge cancer care, fundamental research, advancements in new energy technology and bright young students. The Foundation has donated a very generous amount of funds to the UBC Chem-E-Car team. We would like to recognize the Daniel Family Foundation as the team's Exclusive Diamond Sponsor and extend our special thanks for their outstanding contributions in support of UBC students.

Sponsors

Partner and Biggest Supporter

UBC Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering

The Department provides administrative support, workshop services and lab space for the team to conduct experiments safely and has been extremely supportive of all Chem-E-Car activities.

Gold Sponsor

UBC Faculty of Engineering

UBC Chem-E-Car projects and travel expenses are funded by The Faculty of Applied Science Professional Activities Fund (PAF).

Gold Sponsor

Shell Canada Experiential Learning Fund

The Shell Experiential Learning Fund (SELF) provides funding for University of British Columbia students to participate in enhanced educational experiences. Funding priority is placed on projects related to the Oil and Gas industry and in the Faculties of Engineering and Science (Geosciences) and School of Business.

Silver Sponsor

Precision Nanosystems

Precision Nanosystems is a frontrunner in the development of nanomedicines and genetic modelling. Their technology allows for research to be performed 20X faster than the gold standard systems while also being 10X less expensive.

Silver Sponsor

Botfeeder

Botfeeder is a Toronto-based manufacturer for high-quality 3D printing materials. Their filaments are manufactured in full-compliance with RoHS-regulations and are extensively tested by SGS. Botfeeder has very generously provided us a year's worth of 3D printer filaments for all of our project needs. Check out their products here: https://www.botfeeder.ca/

Silver Sponsor

DigitalOcean

DigitalOcean is a cloud infrastructure provider focused on simplifying web infrastructure for software developers. They have kindly provided support and funding for our hosting and software development needs.

Bronze Sponsor

Free Geek Vancouver

Free Geek is a nonprofit community organization that reduces the environmental impact of waste electronics by reusing and recycling donated technology. If you're in Vancouver, please consider volunteering with them. Free Geek has donated 2 computers to UBC Chem-E-Car as part of a Hardware Grant for non-profit organizations.

Bronze Sponsor

M.G. Chemicals

M.G. Chemicals is a manufacturer and wholesaler of chemical products for the electronics industry. They are a firm believer in the ISO 9001 philosophy of producing quality products that are designed to meet the specific needs of our customers. M.G. Chemicals has kindly sponsored some of their awesome products for our 2016-2017 projects. Thank you!
