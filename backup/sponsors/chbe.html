UBC Chemical and Biological Engineering


The department of Chemical and Biological engineering(CHBE) is has established worldwide acclaim for both its research and graduate quality.
In terms of research, the department is actively producing solutions towards sustainability in the fields of Energy and fuels, Natural resources, Health, and Environment. There are over 300 undergraduates enrolled in the department working towards becoming a professional engineer. A further 175 graduate students are looking into the myriad of programs and specializations that CHBE has to offer such as the new Master of Engineering degree(M.Eng) and various Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degrees.
CHBE provides workshop services and lab space for the team to conduct experiments safely and has been very supportive of all Chem-E-Car activities.
