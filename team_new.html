Team Members

2015/2016 Chem-E-Car Team

Athanasios (Thanos) Kritharis

Chem-E-Car Captain, Electrical Team

Athanasios (Thanos) is currently in his 2nd year of involvement with Chem-E-Car. He originally joined the team reluctantly but, became engrossed in the project. This is his first year as captain and he plans to expand the team and increases its profile so that more students may gain hands on engineering experience.

Michelle Zhou

Safety Officer

Michelle is in her 1st year of involvement with Chem-E-Car and considers team safety to be her top priority. She regularly updates the MSDS and tests equipment to ensure that the lab is up to the most stringent regulation standards. Through her involvement, Chem-E-Car is able to provide all its members the opportunity to learn and collaborate.

Geyi (Johnson) Liu

Electrical Team Lead

Johnson is currently in second year Engineering Physics and is in charge of all electrial design in this team.

Tampriye Asawo

Battery Team Lead, Sponsorship Officer

Tampriye is a third year Chemical Engineering student. She joined Chem-E-Car in 2015 and will be using SolidWorks to design the battery compartment and prepare an appropriate electrolyte solution. She is also involved in administrative duties such as promoting the team. In her spare time, Tampriye enjoys reading and hiking various BC trails.
