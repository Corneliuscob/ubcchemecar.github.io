What We Do

Meet the team. Want to join us? UBC Chem-E-Car welcomes new members from all engineering disciplines as well as students from other faculties.

Note that many of our members on technical sub-teams do contribute to media and administrative duties.

Chemical

Design and test a highly accurate chemical reaction-based car stopping mechanism, i.e. the iodine clock reaction.

Chemical Team

...

Battery

Research potential materials and methods for power sources. Past projects include metal-air batteries and hydrogen fuel cells.

Electrical

Fabricate PCBs (printed circuit boards), design circuitry layout and program Arduino microcontrollers.

Mechanical

Design car body and parts in Solidworks and 3D print designs, making sure it runs reliably with no stuttering and drives in straight line.

Media

Capture photographs and videos for team events. Prepare materials for social media and publicity purposes. Manage sponsorship opportunities.

Administration

Handle the logistics, finance, project management and recruitment of the team.
